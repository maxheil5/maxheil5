TERRASat Project

The Terrain Electromagnetic Reconnaissance and Regional Analysis Satellite (TERRASat) is a 12U CubeSat mission to Mars. It utilizes atmosphere-breathing electric propulsion to measure localized magnetic fields and analyze crustal magnetism. My contributions include mission design, MATLAB-based trajectory modeling, and integrating STK for orbital analysis.
